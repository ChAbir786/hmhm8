Who We Are

One Group.
Multiple Industries.
One Standard.

The HMH Group is a New York-based multi-venture business group operating across recommerce, technology, and retail sectors. We believe in strategic diversification, operational excellence, and building lasting relationships with partners and customers.

Each of our divisions is built on the same core principles: transparency, reliability, and commitment to fair pricing. Whether you're selling medical supplies, diabetic equipment, or smartphones — we ensure competitive offers and fast, hassle-free transactions.

Our network spans the entire United States, backed by professionals dedicated to making recommerce accessible, profitable, and sustainable. We're not just buying and selling — we're building the future of circular commerce.
